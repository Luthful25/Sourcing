Privacy Policy

Empior Sourcing Bangladesh Limited ("Empior", "we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website, social channels, or inquiry forms.

If you do not agree with this policy, please do not access this site.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

a. Personal Information

Information that identifies you as an individual, such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Company name
  • Any other information you provide through our forms or communications

b. Non-Personal Information

Automatically collected data such as:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and device type
  • Operating system
  • Website usage statistics (pages visited, time spent on site, and referral paths)

2. How We Use Your Information

We may use the collected information to:

  • Respond to inquiries and requests submitted through our website
  • Improve our website and customer service
  • Send updates, service-related notices, and communication relevant to your request
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience. These may include:

  • Session cookies for website functionality
  • Analytics cookies for measuring user behavior

You can adjust cookie preferences in your browser settings.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We only share your information when necessary for business operations, legal compliance, or with authorized service providers helping us run our website and communication channels.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • Object to processing for marketing purposes
  • Withdraw consent for data collection at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@empiorsourcing.com.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites.

8. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy as necessary to comply with applicable laws or changes in our operations. Updates will be posted on this page with the effective date.
