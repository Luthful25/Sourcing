Sustainability

Responsible Sourcing and Manufacturing from Bangladesh

Partner With Us

At Empior Sourcing Bangladesh,

We Build A More Sustainable And Responsible Apparel Future

Empior Sourcing Bangladesh is an apparel sourcing and manufacturing partner based in Dhaka, Bangladesh. We are committed to ethical production, environmental care, and transparent collaboration with buyers. Across our programs, we reduce waste, prioritize eco-friendly materials, apply LEAN thinking, and invest in the well-being of our people.

Why Choose Empior As Your Sustainable Bangladesh Partner?

Commitment to Waste Reduction and Environmental Impacts

Empior responsibly manages materials and production planning in Bangladesh to reduce waste and environmental impact while aligning with local laws and international buyer requirements.

Our efforts also include minimizing air emissions and properly handling chemicals to reduce their environmental impact. We prioritize the use of recycled materials in our product development, contributing to a more sustainable supply chain.

For fashion brands sourcing from Bangladesh, Empior helps meet EU and global sustainability expectations through stronger traceability, documentation readiness, and compliance-focused execution.

Wide Range Of Eco-Friendly Materials

One of the core pillars of Empior's sustainability journey in Bangladesh is using eco-friendly materials and process improvements that lower our carbon footprint. Fabrics sourced from trusted partners can be traceable upon request to support future Digital Product Passport (DPP) requirements.

Through our Bangladesh supplier network, we proactively source and develop materials, offering sustainable options like organic cotton, bamboo viscose, recycled polyester, Lenzing TENCEL, and modal, while advising clients to build better-performing products.

Efficiency and Sustainability with LEAN and Kaizen Principles

Our LEAN system focuses on streamlining production processes, eliminating non-value-added activities, and optimizing resource use across all stages of manufacturing. This allows us to deliver high-quality products while reducing environmental impact and improving overall productivity.

Our commitment to Kaizen promotes a culture of continuous improvement, where every team member actively participates in identifying inefficiencies and implementing solutions. Kaizen encourages small, incremental changes that drive long-term sustainability, reduce waste, and improve performance.

Safety and Well-being For Our People

We understand that nurturing a talented and motivated workforce is essential for our growth and sustainability. That is why we prioritize creating a safe and healthy workplace where everyone can thrive.

We are committed to providing fair wages and safe working conditions for all workers, complying with Bangladesh labor laws and buyer code-of-conduct requirements. Empior also embraces diversity and treats every individual with respect and fairness.

Paying Forward For A Better Tomorrow!

With continuous support from communities across Bangladesh, we keep contributing through initiatives such as:

  • Creating stable employment opportunities for families across Dhaka, Gazipur, and Narayanganj.
  • Supporting school supply and digital-learning drives in underserved local communities.
  • Running seasonal relief support for low-income households during flood and winter periods.
  • Partnering with local organizations to promote women's workforce participation and health awareness.

Our Bangladesh Process Setup Is Optimized For Scalable Production

Step 1

Handle Your Inquiry

Share your product brief and targets, and our Bangladesh team will review feasibility, timeline, and the right production path.

Step 2

Prepare Quotation

If your project fits our MOQ and capacity, we provide an initial quotation based on tech pack, selected fabric, trims, and order volume.

Step 3

Sample Development

Once specifications are finalized, our team prepares two types of samples for fit and quality approval — first a 3D sample, then an offline sample.

Step 4

Bulk Production

Following order confirmation and deposit, we launch bulk production with all approved specifications and compliance checkpoints.

Step 5

Quality Control

We apply strict in-line and end-line inspections, including AQL-based final checks, to protect quality consistency.

Step 6

Packaging & Shipping

After final approval, finished goods are packed to buyer requirements and shipped from Bangladesh to your destination.
